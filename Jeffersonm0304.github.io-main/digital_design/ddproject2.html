Our guide to make your own plants

  1. Prepare moistened soil. You will likely have your best success growing your own seeds in seedling soil.
  2. Place soil into cells of tray.
  3. Place 2-3 seeds into each cell.
  4. Allow the seeds to grow.
  5. Remove all but one of the seedlings in each cell.
